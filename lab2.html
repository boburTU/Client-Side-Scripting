Ultimate Fighting Championship

The Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) is an American mixed martial arts organization based in Las Vegas, Nevada, that is owned and operated by parent company WME–IMG. It is the largest MMA promotion in the world and features the top-ranked fighters of the sport. Based in the United States, the UFC produces events worldwide that showcase eleven weight divisions and abide by the Unified Rules of Mixed Martial Arts.

As of 2017, the UFC has held over 400 events. Dana White serves as the president of the UFC. He has held that position since 2001; while under the leadership of Dana White the UFC has grown into a globally popular multibillion-dollar enterprise. The UFC's main market rival is Bellator MMA, which airs on Paramount Network. On network TV, UFC content is available on Fox, Fox Sports 1, and Fox Sports 2 in the U.S., on ESPN in the Caribbean, on BT Sport in the United Kingdom and Ireland, as well as in 150 countries and 22 different languages worldwide.

Top 3 Pound For Pound Fighters of UFC

#1 Rank - Demetrious Johnson

Demetrious Johnson is from Madisonville, Kentucky, USA and is currently the best pound for pound fighter in the UFC. Nicknamed the "Mighty Mouse" and has a fighting record of 27 wins, 2 losses and 1 draw. He is the first and the current Flyweight Champion of UFC and has defended his title 11 times. He is known for his speed, conditioning & technique inside the octagon. He stands 5 foot 3 inches and weighs 125 pounds.

#2 Rank - Conor McGregor

Connor McGregor is from Dublin, Ireland and is currently the 2nd best pound for pound fighter in the UFC. Nicknamed the "The Notorious" and has a fighting record of 21 wins and 3 losses. He is currently the Lightweight Champion of UFC and is a former Featherweight Champion. He is the only fighter to be the champion in two UFC divisions simultaneously. He is known for his speed, technique and knockout power inside the octagon and as a master trash talker outside of the octagon. He stands 5 foot 8 inches and weighs 155 pounds.

#3 Rank - Daniel Cormier

Daniel Cormier is from Lafayette, Lousiana, USA and is currenly the 3rd best pound for pound fighter in the UFC. Nicknamed the "DC" and has a fighting record of 20 wins, 1 losses and 1 no contest. He is currently the Light Heavyweight Champion of UFC and has defended the title 3 times. He is well known for his wrestling inside the octagon and is considered one of the greatest mixed martial artists of all time. He stands 5 foot 11 inches and weighs 205 pounds.
